As of an early age, i have been a big fan of Videogames and just like with most of people my love for videogames started with nintendo.

My first memories of playing video games are from playing games like Mario Kart 64, Mario Party 2 or Super Smash Bros on the N64 together with my cousins and from spending a lot of time with the Wii, DS and Gamecube. I was a proud nintendo kid growing up! Although i also have a lot of memories playing Playstation games like Little Big Planet or Katamari. Growing up on video games, mario has always been my favourite game series. This silly little red plumber from Brooklyn New York made baby miles' brain go "Oh wow videogames quite rock, actually."
