As of an early age, i have been quite a big fan of video games and just like with most of people my love for video games started with nintendo.

My first memories of playing video games are from playing the Nintendo 64, playing games like Mario Kart 64, Mario Party 2 or Super Smash Bros together with my cousins and also from spending a lot of time with consoles like the Wii, DS and GameCube.

I was a proud nintendo kid growing up! But i also have some memories of playing Playstation games like Little Big Planet or Katamari. And when i would be allowed to use the familly Vista PC, i've spend most of the time playing flash games like Super Mario 63.
Looking back, video games were quite a big part of my childhood and they still are to this day!

Growing up on video games, mario has always been my favourite game series. This silly little red plumber from Brooklyn New York made baby miles' brain go "Oh wow videogames quite rule, actually."

And ever since that fateful day, baby miles was never the same again...
